What we're looking for

Email in your portfolio: hireme at sentimech.com

Designers

Forget UML diagrams for a moment. Can you source layouts and architect APIs? Can you explain in detail about the work that you create, before you make it? We regularly contract out our most tedious code. So we need designers that can project manage as well as they code.

Contributors

Do you have a github? Have stars and code for us to look at? Debugging and maintenance can be expensive. So we like reading well commented code that convey quality not quantity. Have you done well in competitions in STEM or sports? Tell us, because being agile with your mental and physical abilities will help us get ahead in this competitive space.

Engineers

At our heart we work with robots with human-like intelligence and creating robots that understand and participate in our world require a physical interface. We need electrical engineers that have love for sensors and pcb designs. We need mechanical engineers that are brave enough to use software to push their designs. Don't have a degree? It doesn't matter, we have the resources to legitamate your inventions.

You are good enough!

We are looking to assemble a small elite team. We want people who are not afraid to make mistakes, who can voice their thoughts, and can pick themselves up after falling down. Do you plan ahead? Can you wake up early in the morning? Do you squander your wealth today or do you invest in the future? Can you stay calm under pressure?

 

Here at SentiMech, you are not limited by your puffed up resume or the source of your education, we care about your portfolio. Whether, big or small, if you have project(s) that demonstrate your talent, we are interested in hiring you and training you!
